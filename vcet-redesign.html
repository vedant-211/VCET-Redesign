About VCET

A Legacy of Knowledge & Innovation

Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology is a premier autonomous engineering institution affiliated to the University of Mumbai. Established under the Vidyavardhini Society — founded in 1970 by the late Padmashri H.G. alias Bhausaheb Vartak — VCET carries a proud tradition of transforming rural education through technological excellence.

Our campus fosters an environment that ignites curiosity, sharpens skills, and nurtures future-ready engineers and managers. With state-of-the-art laboratories, strong industry connections, and a vibrant student community, VCET stands as a beacon of academic rigor and innovation.
